That sounds like an amazing trip! Barcelona is perfect for art, architecture, and nightlife. Here’s a well-balanced 6-day itinerary for you:

### **Day 1: Arrival & Gothic Quarter Exploration**  
- **Morning:** Arrive, check into your accommodation (consider El Born or Eixample for central locations).  
- **Afternoon:** Wander the **Gothic Quarter (Barri Gòtic)**—see the **Barcelona Cathedral**, **Plaça Reial**, and hidden gems like **El Call** (Jewish Quarter).  
- **Evening:** Tapas & drinks at **Bar del Pla** or **El Xampanyet**.  
- **Night:** Cocktails at **Paradiso** (world-famous speakeasy) or live music at **Jamboree**.  

### **Day 2: Gaudí Day – Sagrada Família & More**  
- **Morning:** **Sagrada Família** (book tickets in advance + tower access).  
- **Lunch:** **Federal Café** (great brunch/lunch spot).  
- **Afternoon:** **Casa Batlló** & **La Pedrera** (Gaudí’s masterpieces).  
- **Evening:** Sunset at **Bunkers del Carmel** (panoramic views).  
- **Night:** Drinks at **Terraza Martínez** (seafood & views) or **Dry Martini** (classic cocktail bar).  

### **Day 3: Art & Montjuïc**  
- **Morning:** **Picasso Museum** (book ahead) & stroll through **El Born**.  
- **Afternoon:** **Montjuïc**—visit **Fundació Joan Miró**, **MNAC** (National Art Museum), and **Montjuïc Castle** (great views).  
- **Evening:** Magic Fountain light show (check schedule).  
- **Night:** **Poble Sec** area for tapas (**Quimet & Quimet**) or **La Caseta del Migdia** (chill outdoor bar).  

### **Day 4: Beach Day & Barceloneta Nightlife**  
- **Morning:** Relax at **Bogatell Beach** (less touristy) or **Barceloneta**.  
- **Lunch:** **Can Solé** (authentic seafood paella).  
- **Afternoon:** Rent a bike or explore **Port Vell**.  
- **Evening:** Sunset drinks at **La Fianna** (bohemian vibe).  
- **Night:** Party at **Opium**, **Pacha**, or **Shôko** (beach clubs).  

### **Day 5: Day Trip & Alternative Art**  
- **Option 1:** **Girona & Figueres** (Dali Museum + medieval charm).  
- **Option 2 (if staying in BCN):** **MACBA** (contemporary art), **Sant Antoni Market**, and street art in **Poblenou**.  
- **Evening:** **Razzmatazz** (iconic club) or **Sala Apolo** for indie/electronic.  

### **Day 6: Park Güell & Gràcia**  
- **Morning:** **Park Güell** (early entry for fewer crowds).  
- **Lunch:** **Café Godot** (artsy café in Gràcia).  
- **Afternoon:** Explore **Gràcia** (bohemian boutiques, **Plaça del Sol**).  
- **Evening:** Farewell dinner at **Disfrutar** (high-end tasting menu) or **Tickets** (fun molecular gastronomy).  

### **Extra Tips:**  
- **Transport:** Get a **T-10 metro pass** or **Hola BCN! card** for unlimited rides.  
- **Safety:** Watch for pickpockets in crowded areas.  
- **Nightlife:** Start late (clubs get busy past 1 AM).
