Here’s a tailored 6-day itinerary for your solo trip to Barcelona, blending art, architecture, and nightlife, with safety tips and local insights:

---

### **Day 1: Arrival & Gothic Quarter Exploration**  
- **Morning:** Arrive and check into a solo-friendly neighborhood like **El Born** (historic, central) or **Gràcia** (artsy, relaxed) .  
- **Afternoon:** Wander the **Gothic Quarter**—visit **Barcelona Cathedral** and hidden Roman ruins. Join a free walking tour to meet other travelers .  
- **Evening:** Tapas at **El Xampanyet** (local favorite) or **Mercat Princesa** for a lively market vibe. Try vermouth and patatas bravas .  
- **Nightlife:** Start with cocktails at **Bar Marsella** (absinthe bar since 1820) or live jazz at **Harlem Jazz Club** .  

---

### **Day 2: Gaudí’s Masterpieces**  
- **Morning:** **Sagrada Família** (book timed tickets in advance). Opt for a guided tour to decode its symbolism .  
- **Afternoon:** **Casa Batlló** and **La Pedrera** (skip-the-line tickets recommended). Walk **Passeig de Gràcia** to admire modernist façades .  
- **Evening:** Rooftop drinks at **La Pedrera’s night tour** (stunning views) or **Terraza Martínez** by Montjuïc .  
- **Nightlife:** Dance at **Razzmatazz** (multi-genre club) or flamenco show at **Tablao Cordobés** .  

---

### **Day 3: Montserrat & Wine**  
- **Day Trip:** Take the cogwheel train to **Montserrat Monastery**. Hike to Sant Jeroni for panoramic views .  
- **Afternoon:** Wine tasting at **Oller del Mas** (10th-century castle winery) with tapas .  
- **Evening:** Relax at **El Nacional** (gourmet food hall) or join a **pub crawl** in El Born .  

---

### **Day 4: Art & Beaches**  
- **Morning:** **Picasso Museum** (book ahead) and **MUHBA** for Roman history .  
- **Afternoon:** Bike tour to **Barceloneta Beach** or relax at **Bogatell Beach** (less crowded) .  
- **Evening:** Sunset at **Bunkers del Carmel** (free panoramic views), then tapas in **Gràcia** .  

---

### **Day 5: Modernism & Markets**  
- **Morning:** **Sant Pau Art Nouveau Site** (lesser-known gem) and **Park Güell** (book slots for mosaic areas) .  
- **Afternoon:** **La Boqueria Market** for fresh juices and empanadas. Alternative: **Sant Antoni Market** (local vibe) .  
- **Nightlife:** **Opium Marítim** (beach club) or **Sutton Club** (upscale) .  

---

### **Day 6: Free Day & Departure**  
- **Morning:** Last-minute shopping on **Las Ramblas** or revisit a favorite spot.  
- **Afternoon:** **MNAC** for Catalan art or **Fundació Joan Miró** .  

---

### **Solo Travel Tips**  
- **Safety:** Use cross-body bags, avoid deserted streets at night, and share your location via apps .  
- **Transport:** Get an **Hola BCN! card** for unlimited metro/bus rides .  
- **Meet People:** Join **architecture tours** (e.g., Go City’s Gaudí tour) or tapas crawls .  

For deeper dives into specific attractions, check the linked sources. Enjoy your trip!
