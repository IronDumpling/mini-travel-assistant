Here’s a well-balanced 7-day itinerary for Berlin and Munich in summer 2025, tailored to your budget of **$7,000 for two people**. The plan includes accommodations, transportation, key attractions, and dining, leveraging the best of both cities’ summer events and efficient public transport options.

---

### **Day 1–3: Berlin (3 Nights)**  
**Theme:** History, Culture, and Summer Festivals  
**Budget Allocation:** ~$3,000  

#### **Day 1: Arrival & Iconic Landmarks**  
- **Morning:**  
  - Arrive in Berlin (flight/train options: ~$200–$400 total ).  
  - Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., **Casa Camper Berlin**, ~$170/night ).  
- **Afternoon:**  
  - Visit **Brandenburg Gate** and **Reichstag Dome** (free; reserve dome access in advance).  
  - Lunch at **Street Food auf Achse** (~$30 for two ).  
- **Evening:**  
  - Explore **Museum Island** (celebrating its 200th anniversary with special exhibitions ).  
  - Dinner at **Brauhaus Lemke** (~$60 for two).  

#### **Day 2: History & Outdoor Fun**  
- **Morning:**  
  - **Berlin Wall Memorial** and **East Side Gallery** (free).  
  - Coffee at **The Barn** (~$10).  
- **Afternoon:**  
  - Boat tour on the Spree River (~$50 for two ).  
  - Visit **Pfaueninsel (Peacock Island)** (reopened palace, ~$12 entry ).  
- **Evening:**  
  - Attend the **Berlin Circus Festival** (Tempelhofer Feld, ~$60 for two ).  

#### **Day 3: Summer Festivals & Local Vibes**  
- **Morning:**  
  - Bike tour through **Kreuzberg** (~$40 rental ).  
- **Afternoon:**  
  - **Carnival of Cultures** (June) or **48 Stunden Neukölln** art festival (free ).  
  - Lunch at **Markthalle Neun** (~$40).  
- **Evening:**  
  - Rooftop drinks at **Klunkerkranich** (~$30).  

**Transport in Berlin:**  
- Use the **Deutschland-Ticket** (~€58/month per person for unlimited regional transport ).  

---

### **Day 4–7: Munich (4 Nights)**  
**Theme:** Bavarian Culture, Nature & Summer Events  
**Budget Allocation:** ~$4,000  

#### **Day 4: Travel & Classic Munich**  
- **Morning:**  
  - Take the **ICE train** to Munich (~4 hours, ~$150 for two ).  
  - Check into **25hours Hotel Bikini** (~$150/night ).  
- **Afternoon:**  
  - **Marienplatz** and **Viktualienmarkt** (lunch ~$40).  
- **Evening:**  
  - Dinner at **Hofbräuhaus** (~$70).  

#### **Day 5: Day Trip & Outdoor Adventure**  
- **Morning:**  
  - Train to **Neuschwanstein Castle** (~$100 for two, including entry).  
- **Evening:**  
  - **Sommernachtstraum** fireworks at Olympiapark (~$25 ).  

#### **Day 6: Culture & Local Festivals**  
- **Morning:**  
  - **Nymphenburg Palace** (~$20 entry).  
- **Afternoon:**  
  - **Tollwood Festival** (Olympiapark, free entry ).  
- **Evening:**  
  - **Kocherlball** folk dance at Englischer Garten (free ).  

#### **Day 7: Relax & Departure**  
- **Morning:**  
  - **SEA LIFE Munich** (~$50 for two ).  
- **Afternoon:**  
  - Flight/train back home (~$200–$400).  

**Transport in Munich:**  
- **CityTourCard group ticket** (~€30/day for unlimited travel ).

### **Tips to Save Money**  
1. **Book trains/flights early** for discounts .  
2. **Use public transport** (Deutschland-Ticket covers Berlin-Munich regional trains ).  
3. **Prioritize free events** like street festivals and open-air concerts .
