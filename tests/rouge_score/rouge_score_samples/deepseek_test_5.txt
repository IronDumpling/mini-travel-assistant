Here’s a **14-day backpacking itinerary across Europe** tailored for a **student with a $1500 budget**, focusing on affordable destinations, efficient transport, and budget-friendly experiences.  

---

## **🗺️ Itinerary Overview**  
**Route:** **Prague (Czech Republic) → Budapest (Hungary) → Krakow (Poland) → Bratislava (Slovakia) → Vienna (Austria)**  
✅ **Why this route?**  
- **Affordable cities** (Eastern/Central Europe)  
- **Short, cheap train/bus connections** (avg. $10–$25 per trip)  
- **Hostel-friendly & walkable**  
- **Rich history, nightlife, and free attractions**  

### **💰 Budget Breakdown (Total: ~$1500)**  
| Category       | Estimated Cost | Notes |  
|---------------|--------------|------|  
| **Flights (round-trip)** | $400–$600 | Book early; consider budget airlines like Ryanair  |  
| **Intercity transport** | $150 | Trains/buses (Omio, FlixBus)  |  
| **Hostels (13 nights)** | $250–$350 | Avg. $20–$25/night in dorms  |  
| **Food & Drinks** | $300 | Street food, grocery meals, occasional restaurants  |  
| **Attractions/Extras** | $150 | Free walking tours, budget museums, thermal baths  |  

---

## **📅 Day-by-Day Itinerary**  

### **Days 1–3: Prague, Czech Republic**  
- **Highlights:** Charles Bridge, Old Town Square, Prague Castle (free viewpoints), cheap beer ($2/pint!) .  
- **Budget Tips:**  
  - Free walking tours (tip-based).  
  - Eat at **Lokál** for affordable Czech food.  
  - Stay at **Hostel One Prague** (social, ~$20/night).  

### **Days 4–6: Budapest, Hungary**  
- **Highlights:** Széchenyi Thermal Baths ($20), ruin bars (Szimpla Kert), Fisherman’s Bastion (free views) .  
- **Budget Tips:**  
  - Buy a **Budapest Card** for transport/discounts.  
  - Cook at hostels (Great Market Hall for groceries).  
  - Stay at **Maverick City Lodge** (~$15/night).  

### **Days 7–9: Krakow, Poland**  
- **Highlights:** Wawel Castle, Auschwitz day trip ($25), Kazimierz Jewish Quarter .  
- **Budget Tips:**  
  - Pierogi meals for $4–$6.  
  - Free walking tours (tip guides).  
  - Stay at **Greg&Tom Party Hostel** (~$12/night).  

### **Days 10–11: Bratislava, Slovakia**  
- **Highlights:** Bratislava Castle, UFO Tower viewpoint, cheap Slovak food .  
- **Budget Tips:**  
  - Walk everywhere (compact city).  
  - Try **Bratislavský Meštiansky Pivovar** for local beer.  
  - Stay at **Wild Elephants Hostel** (~$18/night).  

### **Days 12–14: Vienna, Austria**  
- **Highlights:** Schönbrunn Palace gardens (free), Naschmarkt street food, free museum days .  
- **Budget Tips:**  
  - Use **Vienna Pass** if visiting paid attractions.  
  - Picnic in Stadtpark.  
  - Stay at **Hostel Ruthensteiner** (~$25/night).  

---

## **🚆 Transport Between Cities**  
- **Prague → Budapest:** Overnight train (~$30) or FlixBus (~$20) .  
- **Budapest → Krakow:** Train (~$25) or bus (~$15) .  
- **Krakow → Bratislava:** Bus (~$10, 5h) .  
- **Bratislava → Vienna:** Train (~$10, 1h) .  

---

## **💡 Money-Saving Hacks**  
1. **Travel Off-Peak:** Avoid summer crowds; April/May or September are cheaper .  
2. **Eat Like a Local:** Street food (kebabs, burek), supermarket picnics .  
3. **Free Attractions:** Churches, parks, and free museum days (e.g., Vienna’s Kunsthistorisches) .  
4. **Student Discounts:** ISIC card for transport/museum deals .  
5. **Walk/Bike:** Most cities are walkable; rent bikes for ~$10/day .  

---

### **Final Notes**  
- **Flexibility:** Use apps like **Omio** for last-minute bus/train deals .  
- **Safety:** Carry a **padlock** for hostel lockers .  
- **Connectivity:** Get an **Airalo eSIM** for cheap data .
